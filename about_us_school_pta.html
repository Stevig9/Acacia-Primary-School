PARENT-TEACHER ASSOCIATION

The PTA is an organization that strives to make life at Jan van Riebeeck Primary School as enjoyable as possible for every learner, teacher and parent.

The PTA is mainly responsible for organizing sports and social functions, spirit building among the parent corps, and fundraising in addition to that of the Governing Board.

Every year, each class sends approximately two class representatives who serve on the PTA together with three teachers from the school.

Projects coordinated by the PTA rely on the help, ideas and contributions of parents. The class representatives are the links between the school and the parents. Meetings are held 1 – 2 times per term, depending on the scope of the project ahead, but class representatives can alternate each other at the meetings.

We welcome new members to the PTA and encourage parents who have previously served to rejoin us.

Involvement in the PTA offers you the opportunity to be part of the planning of important aspects of the school's functioning, and gives you the chance to provide valuable input. We would like to have you as part of our team: please let your class teacher know if you would like to join us.
